* 高斯公式 `Gauss' law`

  $$\iiint_{\Omega} (\frac{\partial N}{\partial x} + \frac{\partial M}{\partial y} + \frac{\partial R}{\partial z}) \mathrm{d} V = \oiint_{\partial \Omega} N \mathrm{d}y\mathrm{d}z + M \mathrm{d}z\mathrm{d}x + R \mathrm{d}x\mathrm{d}y$$
